<p align="center">
  <b>E-Cars EG</b> is a technology-driven marketplace for electric cars in Egypt — buy, sell, or rent EVs, discover chargers, and explore showrooms.  
  <br/>
  <sub>One platform for all things EV.</sub>
</p>

---

## 🚀 What We Build

- ⚡ EV Marketplace (Buy / Sell / Rent)
- 🔌 EV Chargers Discovery
- 🏢 Showrooms & Partners Network
- 🔐 Security & Anti-Phishing / Anti-Quishing Practices
- 📱 Mobile-first experiences & automation

> _Product direction based on what appears on the official website._
